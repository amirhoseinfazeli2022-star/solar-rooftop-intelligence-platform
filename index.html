About This Prototype

Solar Rooftop Intelligence is an interactive WebGIS dashboard prototype designed to visualize rooftop solar potential and local energy-sharing scenarios at the urban scale.

Data Used

The prototype uses building footprint geometries and a dark basemap to create a spatial interface for rooftop energy analysis.

Estimated Indicators

Rooftop area, PV capacity, annual production, CO₂ avoided and suitability score are simulated indicators used for dashboard demonstration and decision-support logic.

Methodology

Building footprint → rooftop suitability → estimated solar capacity → annual production → surplus/deficit zones → energy-sharing network.

Purpose

The goal is to demonstrate how GIS, spatial data, and dashboard design can support urban energy planning, stakeholder communication, and renewable energy decision-making.

This is a conceptual WebGIS prototype. The current KPI values are not engineering-certified solar calculations, but simulated indicators created to demonstrate spatial analysis, visualization, and dashboard interaction.
